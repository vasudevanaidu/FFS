What is FFS?

Faculty Feedback System is an online site for taking feedback on faculty from students.In which each students of educational institute create their profiles in the system in order to provide their valuable review on teachers who are teaching or mentoring them.




Features



Increase the percentage of genuine feedbacks

Ease of removing regular absentee

Better user interface

Maintenance of genuine feedback





Guidelines

  • 01 Guideline -1

    To see the better results of this system feedback need to be taken from the students with in the time of semester like every 15 or 30 days.

  • 02 Guideline - 2

    Students need to maintain 75% attendance to make changes in their institute through this system.

  • 03 Guideline - 3

    Those who are giving feedback need to utilize this privilages correctly.

  • 04 Guideline - 4

    Only admin can view the results of the feedback.

 

Frequently Asked Questions


  • FAQ - 1

    How to register in this system?
    Each user who had the valid mail can register into this system.

  • FAQ - 2

    How the feedback given by the student are displayed?
    Each question was categorized and each category was displayed as piechart along with it's score in that category.

  • FAQ - 3

    How feedback was taken from the students?
    It's better taken from the students for every 15 or 30 days by giving a notification.

  • FAQ - 4

    Which students are eligible to give feedback?
    Those who had >=75% attendacec percentage can give their feedback.
